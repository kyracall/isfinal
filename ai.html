Sleep When You're Dead

Life is short, I want to live a memorable one, so here are some things that make me feel alive.


  1. Skiing

      My most memorable ski trip was skiing in the Swiss Alps by the Matterhorn. A dream that had been on my bucketlist for years.

  2. Hiking

      The coolest hike I've ever been on was the Havasupai Trail in Arizona.

  3. Serving Others

      I went to Mozambique and helped build a school for these kids, their endless hugs and smiles were the greatest reward.

  4. Running

      I ran a half marathon with my family in 2024. The race day adrenaline was real!

  5. Worshiping in The Temple

      I feel closer to my Savior Jesus Christ when I enter His holy house. ♥

  6. Driving with the Windows Down

      Once a passenger princess, always a passenger princess.

  7. Human Connection

      While serving a mission in the Philippines, I found out me and this cute nanay have the same birthday.

  8. Concerts

      Went to Taylor Swift's Era's Tour with my sisters, childhood dream come true!

  9. Jumping Out of a Plane

      I went skydiving for my 18th birthday, coolest experience ever!

  10. Dance Parties in Lake Powell

      Daily dance parties are good for the soul. A close second being the feeling of washing your hair in the lake.
